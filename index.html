Born Sivaraj Manickam and fighting out of Singapore, Khalid Shakur made his professional debut on 15 February 2025 — entering the middleweight division with precision, purpose, and nothing to lose. At 29, he arrived with the sharpness of a seasoned competitor and a hunger no younger fighter could match.

Three fights in, three wins — including two knockouts — Shakur is already ranked #1 in Singapore and cutting through the region's middleweight scene with the controlled aggression that earned him his name: The Sword.
